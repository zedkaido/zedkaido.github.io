Offline

Having access to the internet is a blessing and a curse. In a way you have everything you ever need at your fingertips. But, it can also keep you distracted - hooked to the infinite stream of data, blobbed in all kinds of shapes and forms to keep you dependent.

Do you control the machine when browsing?
Or, are you being controlled by the machine?

The easiest way to re-gain control is to turn your internet off. And, if you are not courageous enough, at least turn off access to the problematic websites that keep you from achieving great things.
